About

Currently, I'm a Postdoctoral Scholar with Dr. Patrick Purdon in the department of Anesthesiology, Perioperative and Pain Medicine at Stanford University.
I work on computational models for treatment optimization in anesthesia and pain management. My projects include causal inference of average and individual treatment effects of surgical pain management strategies, Transformer models for interpretable predictions using diagnosis and procedure billing codes, and physiologically informed dynamic models of postoperative pain.
Previously, I completed both my PhD in Biomedical Engineering (2021) and my BS in Biomedical Engineering (2016) at Johns Hopkins University.
