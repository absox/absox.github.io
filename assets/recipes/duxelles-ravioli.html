Feeds four.

Ingredients:

Pasta:

  • 280g flour
  • 6 eggs

Duxelles Filling:

  • Cremini mushrooms, 1 lb
  • 2 shallots
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Thyme

Mornay Sauce:

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Chives
  • Parmagiano reggiano cheese
  • Milk

Instructions:

  1. Knead 4 egg yolks and 2 eggs into 280g of flour until homogeneous. Wrap in plastic wrap and let rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare duxelles. Blend mushrooms, shallots, garlic, and thyme into a fine paste. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook duxelles in a nonstick pan over dry heat until excess moisture is gone.
  4. Using a pasta machine, roll pasta dough into thin sheets, and assemble raviolis.
  5. Cook raviolis in salted boiling water for 2 minutes.
  6. Prepare mornay sauce. Create a blond roux, then stir in milk until the desired consistency is achieved for bechamel sauce.
  7. Stir parmagiano reggiano into the bechamel on a low flame to create mornay sauce, pour a thin layer over raviolis in a soup dish.
